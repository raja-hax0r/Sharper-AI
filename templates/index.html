📖 COGNITIVE MANUAL

Welcome to Pariah's Mineshaft, corporate worker. When your thinking begins to feel secondary to AI generation, it is time to dig.

1. Mineshafts (Classes): Each shaft represents a different category of business thinking. Coal is arithmetic; Iron is chart reading; Gold is optimization; Ruby is logical matching; Diamond is complex multi-class business scenarios.

2. Daily & Weekly Mines: The Daily Mine generates a medium-difficulty problem of any class once every 24 hours. The Weekly Expedition generates an intricate, multi-layered problem once a week. Solving these rewards major gems.

3. Attempts Limits: Be careful! The mineshaft will cave in and lock you out if you guess too many times. Easy sites (Coal) give you 9 attempts. Difficulty increments subtract 1 attempt (Iron = 8, Gold = 7, Ruby = 6, Diamond = 5). Max attempts locks the puzzle permanently.

4. Gems & Hints: Buying progressive clues costs 3 Gems. They help you solve without spoiling the answer. Solving questions earns gems.

5. Drillin AI Widget: Click the floating button in the bottom right corner to communicate with Drillin (Connection cost: 2 gems). He clarifies question parameters before solving, and reviews your notes to help you explain numbers to your boss.
